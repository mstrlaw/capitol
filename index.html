What's this?

A project to collect and provide a dynamic way of viewing the events surrounding and leading to the 2021 storming of the U.S. Capitol.

How does it work?

Navigate the map and click on clusters or individual markers to view the collected videos for that location.

Can I help?

Yes! Help us pinpoint the geo location of the videos and tag their content. See here how to do it.

Why do this?

These events represent an assault on democratic values in the U.S. and all around the world. This is our way of making sure this day is not forgotten.
